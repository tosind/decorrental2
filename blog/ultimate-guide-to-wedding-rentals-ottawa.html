The Ultimate Guide to Wedding Rentals in Ottawa (2026)

Published February 2026 | Ottawa Decor Rentals

Planning a wedding in Ottawa is an exciting journey — and one of the best decisions you can make is to rent your decor rather than buying it. Renting saves money, reduces waste, and gives you access to a higher quality of items than you could typically afford to purchase outright. This guide covers everything you need to know.

Why Rent Your Wedding Decor?

  • Cost-effective: Rental prices are a fraction of the retail cost, especially for items like candelabras, charger plates, or lounge furniture.
  • Less stress: The rental company handles clean-up and collection — you don't have to figure out what to do with 200 charger plates after your wedding.
  • Eco-friendly: Reusable items mean less waste in Ottawa's landfills.
  • Wider selection: Access a professional curator's inventory of premium items you'd never find at a big-box store.

What Can You Rent for a Wedding in Ottawa?

Virtually everything decorative can be rented. Here's a breakdown of the most popular categories:

Linens & Table Decor

Tablecloths, runners, napkins, charger plates, candle holders, vases, and place card holders. Linen choices alone can dramatically transform the look of a reception room.

Lounge & Seating Furniture

Velvet sofas, accent chairs, love seats, ottomans, and coffee tables are increasingly popular for creating elegant cocktail-hour lounge vignettes or photo-worthy corners at your reception.

Ceremony Arches & Stands

From geometric gold frames to rustic wooden arches, these pieces form the visual centrepiece of your ceremony space and pair beautifully with fresh or dried floral arrangements.

Lighting & Ambiance

Candelabras, lanterns, pillar candles, votive holders, fairy lights, and string light arrangements create warmth and romance that no venue lighting system can replicate.

How Far in Advance Should You Book?

In Ottawa, popular wedding dates (especially May–October Saturdays) book up quickly. We recommend reaching out to your rental supplier at least 3–6 months in advance to secure your preferred items. Our most-requested bundles, like the Micro Wedding Luxe and Garden Romance packages, are particularly high-demand.

DIY Pickup vs. Full Styling Service

At Ottawa Decor Rentals, we offer two ways to work with us:

  • DIY Pickup: You collect and return the items yourself. More hands-on, but very cost-effective.
  • Done-For-You (DFY): Our team delivers, sets up, and tears down. Best if you want to enjoy your day completely stress-free.
