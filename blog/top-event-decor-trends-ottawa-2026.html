Top Event Decor Trends in Ottawa for 2026

Published February 2026 | Ottawa Decor Rentals

Planning a celebration in Ottawa this year? Whether you're organizing a wedding, a graduation party, a bridal shower, or a corporate gala, the world of event decor is evolving beautifully in 2026. As Ottawa's premier rental supplier, we've seen these trends first-hand and we're excited to share them with you.

1. Romantic Maximalism

Gone are the days of stark minimalism ruling the event space. In 2026, Ottawa couples and event hosts are embracing romantic maximalism — layering florals, candles, textures, and statement pieces to create lush, deeply immersive environments. Think dramatic floral arches, cascading candlelight, velvet linens, and multiple decorative focal points throughout a venue.

2. Sustainable & Organic Materials

Ottawa's environmentally-conscious community is pushing for greener celebrations. This means renting over buying is more popular than ever (a great reason to choose decor rentals!), and couples are choosing organic-looking greenery, dried pampas grass, terracotta pottery, and naturally woven runners over mass-produced plastic decor.

3. Bold Colour Palettes Making a Comeback

Sage and beige have had their moment. The 2026 Ottawa event scene is embracing bold, saturated colour palettes: deep coral, cobalt blue, forest green, and warm terracotta are appearing on tables and arches alike. Paired with gold or brass metallic accents, these colours create a richly memorable aesthetic.

4. Personalized Tablescapes

Guests are no longer satisfied with generic table settings. Hosts are working with stylists to create highly personalized and curated tablescapes — custom name cards, locally-sourced floral arrangements, and mix-and-match charger plates that tell a visual story unique to the couple or host.

"A table setting should feel like a piece of art that your guests get to sit inside." — Ottawa Decor Rentals Styling Team

5. Luxury Outdoor Experiences

Ottawa's stunning parks, golf estates, and riverside locations are perfect for elevated outdoor events. Luxury picnics, outdoor receptions in gatineau and Rideau-area estates, and garden parties complete with lounge furniture and lantern lighting are among the fastest-growing requests we receive.

6. Interactive Decor Elements

Modern events are incorporating decor that guests can interact with — floral photo backdrops, lounge vignettes with plush seating and coffee table books, or even self-serve cocktail stations styled with bespoke barware and greenery. These features serve double duty as decor and entertainment.
