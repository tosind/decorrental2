Top 5 Event Decor Trends in Ottawa for 2025

Planning a wedding or private event in Ottawa? Staying ahead of the trends can ensure your celebration is unforgettable. At Ottawa Decor Rentals, we provide premium vases, linens, luxury seating, and customized bundles to bring these trends to life.

1. The Return of Vintage Romance

Expect to see more couples opting for timeless, vintage-inspired decor. Think ornate gold candelabras, lace overlays, and elegant charger plates. Our rental catalog features a wide selection of vintage-inspired pieces perfect for micro weddings and grand receptions alike.

2. Luxury Picnic Experiences

Outdoor celebrations are taking a sophisticated turn. Our luxury picnic bundles, complete with low boho tables, plush cushions, and intricate floral centerpieces, are becoming the go-to choice for bridal showers and intimate anniversaries in Ottawa's beautiful parks and backyards.

3. Minimalist Elegance

Less is sometimes more. Minimalist table settings featuring clean lines, modern cutlery, and single-stem florals in minimalist vases are highly sought after. We offer sleek, modern rentals that perfectly complement this aesthetic.

4. Bold, Moody Color Palettes

While pastels will always have their place, darker, moodier color palettes (deep emeralds, rich burgundies, and navy) paired with metallic accents are making a stunning entrance into event styling.

5. Done-For-You Styling Services

With busy schedules, more clients are seeking "Done-For-You" services. Beyond just DIY rentals, our full-service styling ensures every detail, from the draped linens to the lit candles, is perfectly executed on your big day.
