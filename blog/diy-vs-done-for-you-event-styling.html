DIY vs Done-For-You: Which Event Styling Option Is Right for You?

Published February 2026 | Ottawa Decor Rentals

At Ottawa Decor Rentals, every one of our bundles offers two booking modes: DIY Pickup and Done-For-You (DFY). It's one of our most-loved features, because every client has different needs, budgets, and timelines. But how do you choose? Let's break it down.

What Is DIY Pickup?

With our DIY option, you pick up your rental items from our Ottawa location, set them up yourself at your venue, and return everything after your event. You have full creative control over the setup, and this option comes at a notably lower price point.

What Is Done-For-You (DFY)?

Our DFY service means our professional styling team delivers everything to your venue, sets it up completely according to your vision, and returns to tear it all down after the event is over. You don't lift a finger — except to enjoy yourself.

Side-by-Side Comparison

Factor DIY Pickup Done-For-You
Cost Lower (you provide labour) Higher (includes delivery + setup + teardown)
Flexibility Very flexible — you control the styling Collaborative — you guide, we execute
Stress Level Moderate (requires coordination on event day) Low — relax and enjoy your event
Best For Couples or hosts with helping hands available Busy couples and those wanting zero day-of hassle
Logistics You transport and return items We handle all delivery, setup, and collection

Who Should Choose DIY?

DIY is a great fit if you're hosting a smaller, backyard event where setup logistics are simple, or if you have a team of friends and family who are eager to help. It's also ideal if you want to keep costs down and you're comfortable with a bit of event-day coordination.

Who Should Choose Done-For-You?

If it's your wedding day, a milestone birthday, a corporate function, or any event where you simply cannot afford to be running around setting up tablescapes, DFY is absolutely worth the investment. Our team ensures every vase, candle, and linen is placed exactly as planned, so that when your guests walk in the room, they're instantly transported.
