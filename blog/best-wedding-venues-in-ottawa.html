Best Wedding Venues in Ottawa & the Surrounding Area (2026)

Published February 2026 | Ottawa Decor Rentals

Ottawa and the surrounding region are home to some of Ontario and Quebec's most breathtaking wedding venues — from Gatineau's rolling hills to Rideau-Valley estate farms and Ottawa's own historic architecture. As a decor rental supplier who regularly works across the region, we've styled events at many of these locations and have some informed opinions to share.

Urban Ottawa Venues

The Château Laurier

For couples seeking old-world grandeur, the Fairmont Château Laurier remains an iconic choice. Its castle-like stone exterior and opulent interior ballrooms pair magnificently with classic romantic decor — think tall gold candelabras, crystal glassware, and dramatic floral centrepieces.

The Cartier Place Suite Hotel

A more boutique downtown option, popular for intimate receptions and cocktail-style events. Its modern interior receives our velvet lounge furniture and boho-contemporary tablescapes very well.

The RA Centre

A versatile event centre with multiple hall sizes for groups of any scale, with excellent kitchen facilities and proximity to convenient Ottawa transit.

Outdoor & Garden Venues Near Ottawa

Estate Farms in Manotick & Almonte

The villages of Manotick and Almonte, within 30–45 minutes of downtown Ottawa, are home to several private estates and heritage farms that host weddings. These are perfect for maximalist outdoor garden reception setups with floral arches, farm tables, and string lighting.

Gatineau Park Pavilions

Gatineau Park on the Quebec side of the Ottawa River offers several natural and semi-covered pavilions that serve as stunning ceremony venues, especially in summer and early fall when the foliage is dramatic.

Rideau Valley Properties

Properties along the Rideau Waterway offer sweeping riverside and lakeside backdrops. Rustic barn weddings flourish here, perfectly complemented by our farmhouse and boho styling inventory.

What to Ask Your Venue Before You Book Rentals

  • What is the load-in and load-out window for vendors?
  • Are candles and open flames permitted?
  • Is there a preferred vendor list we need to adhere to?
  • Is there parking for our delivery vehicle?
  • What are the backup plans for inclement weather?
