Luxury Picnic Ideas for Ottawa Parks & Backyards (2026 Guide)

Published February 2026 | Ottawa Decor Rentals

The luxury picnic trend has fully arrived in Ottawa — and it's showing no signs of slowing down in 2026. What began as a social-media-driven aesthetic has evolved into one of the most popular celebration formats for bridal showers, anniversaries, birthday parties, and milestone proposals across the city.

What Is a Luxury Picnic?

A luxury picnic is a styled, intimate outdoor gathering that replaces the classic checkered blanket with a curated, design-forward setup. Think: low boho tables with plush floor cushions, candles glowing in glass holders, overflowing charcuterie and floral arrangements, and a cohesive colour palette running from the tablerunner to the balloon garland.

Best Spots for a Luxury Picnic in Ottawa

  • Gatineau Park – Stunning wilderness backdrop, especially in spring and summer.
  • Commissioners Park – Lakeside views and the iconic Dows Lake setting.
  • Strathcona Park – Lush greenery close to the Rideau River. Beautiful in warmer months.
  • Major's Hill Park – Right in the heart of Ottawa with Parliament Hill as your backdrop.
  • Private Backyards – For a more intimate and private setup, your backyard (or a family member's estate) can be perfectly transformed.

What to Rent for Your Luxury Picnic

The beauty of renting is that all the setup and teardown is handled, so you can focus on enjoying the moment. For a luxury picnic, popular rental items include:

  • Low boho picnic tables and floor cushions or poufs
  • Table runners in natural linen, macramé, or velvet
  • Candle holders, lanterns, and fairy light strings
  • Vases and bud vases for floral arrangements
  • Decorative plates, glasses, and napkins
  • A floral or balloon backdrop for photos

Seasonal Picnic Styling Tips for Ottawa

Spring & Summer: Bright florals, organic linens, pastel tones, and fresh floral garlands work beautifully in Ottawa's warm months. Plan for a backup in case of afternoon showers — parks can be unpredictable.

Fall: Embrace rich warm tones like burnt orange, deep burgundy, and gold. Dried floral arrangements, terracotta vases, and cable-knit throws add warmth and texture.

Winter / Indoor: Yes, luxury indoor "picnics" are a thing! Set up in your living room or a rented event space with cozy throws, string lights, and floor cushions for a hygge-inspired celebration.
